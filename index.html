ABOUT PETROGRAD

Petrograd is a restaurant opened in 2007 in the core center of Copenhagen. It has moved its location through the years and that way it reached more and more people. It's a restaurant that welcomes big groups of people as much as it does one person. It has a cool dinamic scandinavian style while offering Russian food.
The restuarents owner is Eva Malek, a 19 yr old from Zagreb, Croatia with passion for different cuisine.
It all started as a small plan to make a food truck and turned into a "traveling" restaurant.

"I would say that my best advice to aspiring chefs is: Suck it up! In this generation, many young cooks are inspired by getting well-known very fast and by living on top of their heads rather than in their bodies."

Chef, Owner of Lee restaurants: chef Susur Lee

Opening hours: everyday from 10am to 8pm

Address: Vognporten 14, 2620 Albertslund
